Best animated movies

Pixar

Up is a heartwarming tale about adventure, loss, and unexpected friendships. Released in 2009, it won two Academy Awards.

Disney Legends

Frozen (2013) redefined Disney musicals for a new generation, with its strong sisterly bond and unforgettable songs.

Other Gems

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse brought a fresh comic-book animation style and a multiverse of web-slingers in 2018.
